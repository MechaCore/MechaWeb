Rate Limting:

Mecha has shards (even without being verified) that hold your server in check. The change of rate limits are very unique compared to other bots. Even though it IS possible to push Mecha's limits, we suggest you don't.

Loopholes:

Mecha has no known loopholes. But if you find one, please report it to our staff team so we can patch it. Loopholes are a VITAL part of Mecha as this can crush our development as well as the continuation of updates.
